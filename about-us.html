Our Vision

At Resevoo, we have a vision to transform the reservation system within UAE's hectic hospitality industry. We understand the decision of finding the best venue for your daytime and evening plans is only becoming increasingly more difficult with the plethora of restaurants, and beach clubs opened and still opening in this hospitality hotspot of the world. Therefore, we aim to help every user find the most suitable venue for their needs with our advanced reservation application. The features which make Resevoo an exceptional and unique booking system include a 3D virtual tour of each restaurant and highlights of a venue's ambience, allowing you to fully understand what you should expect at your venue of choice.

Our Story

Resevoo was launched on November 1st, 2024, by a young and innovative multicultural team with forward thinking ideas for an industry desperate for fresh thinking and imagination. At Resevoo, risk and originality are our driving forces. We have three founders of Emirati, British and Bengali heritage. We believe that the vast difference in our cultures is what allows us to relate to the wide-ranging customer base in the experience industry. Within the dynamic Resevoo team, the goal is clear: creating the greatest, most efficient and innovative reservation app is our mission.

About Resevoo

Resevoo is not just a reservation app - Resevoo is the hospitality hub of the UAE. Whether we guide you to your perfect venue through our application or our social media handles, our aim is to always offer a new way to enjoy this beautiful country's range of fine dining restaurants, and vibrant beach clubs.

What sets Resevoo apart from other reservation apps?

Resevoo distinguishes itself from other applications through our extensive range of features and the high caliber of clients with whom we partner. Our distinctive features range from selecting a preferred waiter language, the ability to pre-order your food before arrival, our 3D tour feature, as well as our highlights and reels options that allow customers to see the very best of all venues within the app. At Resevoo, we believe that to be the best you must partner and collaborate with the best. Thus, we only partner with high-end restaurant groups to ensure exceptional food quality and top-notch service for our users.
